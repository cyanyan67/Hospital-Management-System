All visitors must follow VitaCore Hospital’s COVID-19 Screening Policy and fully cooperate with the Hospital Staff to prevent and control the infection.
Only ONE (1) visitor is allowed at a time for a duration of 15 minutes.
A maximum of THREE (3) visitors are allowed per patient per day.
Children aged 12 years and below are not allowed.
All visitors must be fully vaccinated.
Visitors must wear a face mask, keep at least 1 meter distance from patients, sanitize hands often, and are encouraged to wear a face shield.
Visitor shall confine their movement within the patient’s room.
The stay-in companion is allowed to stay with the patient during visiting hours. Always wear mask and maintain 1 meter distance from other visitors.
COVID-19 saliva test is recommended before visiting for safety of our patients. Please quarantine at home if tested POSITIVE on saliva test.
The Security Guard reserves all rights to escort any visitor from the ward if found non-compliant with the above said policy.
